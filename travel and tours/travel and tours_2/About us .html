ExploreNow Travel & Tours is a leading Ugandan travel company dedicated to making travel accessible, affordable, and adventurous. Our team is passionate about creating personalized journeys that leave lasting memories.

Core Objectives

Customer Satisfaction. Ensuring travelers have a positive and memorable experience.
Convenience. Offering one-stop services (flights, hotels, tours, insurance, etc.).
Tourism Promotion.Promoting tourist destinations and cultural experiences.
Revenue Generation. Through commissions, packages, and direct service charges

Purpose

To make travel easy, enjoyable, and stress-free by handling the planning, booking, logistics, and sometimes even guiding aspects of a trip.
